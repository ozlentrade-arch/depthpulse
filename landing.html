Do I need a broker account?+
For live data you'll connect your existing Rithmic or CQG broker account — both are supported by most futures brokers. The demo app works without any account.
Does it work on Mac and iPad?+
Yes — fully browser-based. Works on any device with a modern browser. Chrome, Safari, Firefox all supported. The Pro plan includes a PWA you can install to homescreen on iOS and Android.
How is this different from Jigsaw or Quantower?+
Jigsaw is DOM-only, desktop-only, $197/mo. Quantower has great tools but requires Windows installation. MotiveWave is powerful but complex and desktop-only. DEPTHPULSE combines all major orderflow tools in one browser-based platform — works on Mac, Windows, and iPad from day one.
